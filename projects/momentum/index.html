Momentum

Momentum is a 3D third-person platformer that pushes your reflexes and combat skills to the limit.

You’ll run, jump, and fight your way through a series of challenging levels filled with deadly gaps and even deadlier enemies.

It’s not just about getting from point A to B, it’s about surviving what’s in between.

As you progress, you’ll also be able to unlock new abilities to boost your mobility and give you the edge in battle.
