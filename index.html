Hex Studios

Hex Studios is an indie game development studio based in Europe, currently ran by a solo developer from Italy.

Our main focus is making games that the community may find original and engaging, with cool and fun gameplay mechanics, as well as interesting lore.

We generally like to blend various genres and themes, shaping them into our own concept.

Our latest project is Momentum, a 3D third-person platformer.
